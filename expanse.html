THE GREAT EXPANSE

What the heck is the great expanse?

Imagine a game where you can do what you want to influence the world around you without being held back by linear borders. Where you can travel across space with the freedom of choosing what you want to do next. The Great Expanse is the world we aim to build to bring you this experience. It is a sector of space loaded with solar systems, stations, planets, wrecks, asteroid fields - whatever; that is waiting to be explored and interacted with by you. We are working as fast as we can to deliver the Expanse to your doorstep, but as you might imagine - designing something as large and complex as a galaxy takes time, planning, modeling, and a lot of code.

We are constantly hard at work on creating and planning out the Expanse, but we will do our best here to give you an idea on what you might expect to see once our work is complete.
