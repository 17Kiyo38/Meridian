Section 1: What is Expanse / E:S

Where and how do I play Expanse or E:S

Expanse and E:S are available on PC only and hosted on www.roblox.com at this link. By clicking the green Play button on the website, you will load into the game's main menu where you can choose between playing Expanse and E:S, respectively. By clicking on the game's own play button, you will once again reload into the game of your choice.

Section 2: What do I do in Expanse / E:S

What kind of occupations can I get into?

Within Expanse, we hope to create a universe where any possible occupation that would give the player a sense of purpose within the game is an occupation you should be allowed to choose. Jobs such as a barber or grocery store clerk probably won't be available but others such as miners, civil servants, or even if you want to go the criminal path, are all planned to be a choice. Some possibilities may include, but are not limited to: Military, Industrial, Trading, Exploration, and Criminal.

Can I customize my ships?

Ships can be customized with different kinds of liveries that we have made available within the storefront. These liveries are sectioned into different rarities and purchased from a randomized lootbox system that give different rarities different percentages to appear. Liveries will only appear for ships you own. These can be purchased with EM in E:S. To equip these liveries, go to the shipyard, select the ship you acquired it for, and click on the "Skins" Submenu and your list of skins will appear on the bottom scroller and clicking on your product will equip it.

How will in-game factions/alliances work in the future?

Factions are currently being planned out and any updates to its setup will be posted to our Roadmap

How does the economy work?

The economy is currently being planned out and any updates to its production will be posted to our Roadmap

Will there ever be any significant out-of-ship content in Expanse (including but not limited to planet exploration, vehicle driving, etc.)?

Stations will be available to roam around and have services available but that's all thats currently planned.

How does the Starmap work?

The starmap is constantly changing and over time the warp gates connecting different systems will soon connect to a different one depending on their distance from each other. Check out this gif to see a glimpse of what this means.

Will there be any ship to environment combat in Expanse? Like for example, you’d take down the turrets to a base or take down shield generators

That has not been thoroughly planned but we will keep it updated on our Roadmap. Currently, the plan is that environment cannot be destroyed other than through mining and other occupational means.

Section 3: Other Information

Will there be many more game modes and how much info can you give us on them?

We aren't looking to add more mechanics on Skirmisher after release. Everything that's coded in will be all there is.

Will there be better coordinated combat in the future? For example, fighters could form squadrons and take out battleships quicker

We hope players will do that on their own because it's something that is currently doable, but it's difficult to manage with people you don't know.

Will there be a ship roadmap anywhere so we can see what’s to come?

A roadmap will soon be added to the website to cover all of our work including ships and other content.
